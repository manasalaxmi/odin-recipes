Margherita Pizza

Description

A classic Italian pizza made with simple ingredients — fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil — baked on a crispy thin crust.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Spread tomato sauce on rolled-out dough.
  3. Add mozzarella slices and fresh basil leaves.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle salt.
  5. Bake for 10–12 minutes until crust is golden.
