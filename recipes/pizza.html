Description

This homemade pizza recipe will give you a crispy, flavorful crust and delicious toppings. Perfect for a weekend treat!

You can customize it with your favorite toppings like cheese, vegetables, pepperoni, or olives.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast, and salt. Add warm water and olive oil. Knead into dough.
  2. Let the dough rise for about 1 hour until doubled in size.
  3. Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F).
  4. Roll out the dough, spread pizza sauce, and sprinkle cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Slice and serve hot!
