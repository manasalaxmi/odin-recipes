Chickpea Salad

Description

This fresh chickpea salad is quick, healthy, and perfect for a light lunch or as a side dish. Packed with protein, fiber, and vibrant flavors.

You can easily adjust the ingredients to your taste or add extra veggies like cucumbers or peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas (drained & rinsed)
  • 1 small red onion (finely chopped)
  • 10-12 cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: feta cheese, cucumbers, olives

Steps

  1. Combine chickpeas, onion, tomatoes, and parsley in a large bowl.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour dressing over the salad ingredients and toss gently to coat.
  4. Top with feta cheese or olives if desired.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 2 days.
