Chickpea Salad

Description

This refreshing chickpea salad is healthy, protein-packed, and perfect for a light lunch or side dish.

Ingredients

  • Cooked chickpeas
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Fresh parsley
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables and herbs.
  2. Mix chickpeas, tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and parsley.
  3. Add lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss well and serve chilled.
