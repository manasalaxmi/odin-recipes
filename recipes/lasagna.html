Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a delicious, layered pasta dish with cheese and rich tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles, prepare sauce, layer ingredients.
  3. Bake for 45 minutes. Let cool before serving.
